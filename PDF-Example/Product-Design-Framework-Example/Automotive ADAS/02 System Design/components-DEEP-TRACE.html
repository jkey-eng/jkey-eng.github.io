    • Lane Detection ADAS-SYS-FEAT-1

      Lane Detection is a feature designed to enhance road safety by monitoring lane markings on the road. It detects when a vehicle begins to unintentionally drift out of its lane and provides alerts or corrective actions, such as steering adjustments, to keep the vehicle centered. This functionality is crucial for preventing accidents caused by driver fatigue, distraction, or inattention, particularly during highway driving.
      • Lane Detection ADAS-SYS-REQ-1

        The ADAS System shall provide the means to determine when a car is drifting from a lane while driving.
        • Driver Drifts Out of Lane ADAS-SYS-CASE-1

          A Driver is driving their Vehicle down the road at speeds above 35mph and starts falling asleep. The ADAS system should automatically steer the Vehicle back to the center of the lane and provide a warning to the Driver.
      • ADAS Camera Sensor ADAS-SYS-REQ-2

        The ADAS system shall include a forward-facing camera sensor capable of capturing high-resolution images in real-time to support lane detection, object recognition, and traffic sign identification.
